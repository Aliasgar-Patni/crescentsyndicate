Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for contractors and procurement teams.

What scaffolding accessories do you supply? +

We supply 27+ products including tie rods (15mm, 17mm), 2-wing anchor nuts, wing nuts, cuplock top & bottom cups, ledger blades, forged fix couplers, swivel couplers, sleeve couplers, base jacks, U head jacks, vertical & ledger pipes, prop sleeves, LT props, shuttering clamps, GOGO clamps, water stoppers and more. Full range available for scaffolding and formwork systems.

Do you offer wholesale / bulk pricing? +

Yes. Crescent Syndicate is a direct supplier — no middleman markups. We offer competitive wholesale and bulk pricing on all scaffolding accessories. The more you order, the better the rate. Call +91 8838006319 or WhatsApp us for a bulk quote within 1 hour.

Which states do you deliver to? +

We supply and deliver scaffolding accessories across all of South India — Tamil Nadu, Kerala and Karnataka Our base is in Coimbatore with fast dispatch to any job site.

What sizes of tie rods are available? +

We stock tie rods in 15mm and 17mm diameters with standard lengths of 1m and 1.5m. Custom lengths are also available on order. Our tie rods are made from high-grade steel (Fe415) with precision metric threading, compatible with standard wing nuts and anchor nuts.

Do your couplers meet IS / BS EN 74 standards? +

Yes. Our forged fix couplers and swivel couplers meet BS EN 74 standards. All scaffolding accessories are manufactured using quality materials and tested for load-bearing capacity before dispatch. Quality assurance is a core commitment at Crescent Syndicate.

How quickly can I get a quote? +

WhatsApp us at +91 8838006319 and get a quote within 1 hour during business hours (Mon–Sat, 9 AM–8 PM IST). For large bulk orders, we respond within 24 hours with a detailed quotation. You can also download our product catalogue above.
